Technology

How KI-Beleuchtung Analyzes Your Music

1

Real-time audio capture

KI-Beleuchtung captures the audio signal directly from your player output or from your computer's microphone. Analysis starts as soon as sound is detected, with no prior configuration.

2

Spectral analysis & BPM

The analysis engine breaks down the audio signal into frequency bands: bass (20–250 Hz), mids (guitars, vocals) and highs (drums, cymbals). BPM is calculated continuously by detecting rhythmic energy peaks.

3

Color & intensity generation

Based on dominant frequencies and overall signal energy, the AI generates Farbe combinations coherent with the Musikal mood. An energetic track triggers warm, intense Farbes; a ballad produces cool, soft tones.

4

Automatic DMX output

Calculated values are sent directly to MyStrow's DMX engine and transmitted in real time over your Art-Net line. All your fixtures react to the Musik continuously, with no manual intervention.

Analyzed Parameters

  • Bass frequencies (20–250 Hz)
  • Mid frequencies (250 Hz – 4 kHz)
  • High frequencies (4 kHz – 20 kHz)
  • BPM and Tempo (40–200 BPM)
  • Global RMS level
  • Energy variations (transitions, breaks)

AI Features

An Autonomous Lighting Brain for Your Stage

🤖

Autonomous stage control

In KI-Beleuchtung mode, MyStrow automatically handles all your DMX fixtures. Each of the 6 groups (A to F) receives individual instructions based on its scenic role, across up to 4 Art-Net universes (2,048 channels). The AI differentiates ambient lighting from accents and effects.

🎵

BPM & rhythm analysis

The BPM engine synchronizes light effects to the Musik Beats: flash on the kick, intensity variation on chords, light transitions locked to Musikal dynamics.

🎨

Intelligent Farbe generation

The algorithm produces coherent Farbe palettes that evolve throughout the track and change dramatically on major transitions (chorus, break, outro).

🛠️

Programming assistance

Launch a track, activate AI, record the generated sequences on your timeline. You get a programming base to refine keyframe by keyframe.

Comparison

KI-Beleuchtung or Manual Programming?

KI-Beleuchtung Mode

  • Ideal for shows without rehearsal
  • Instant reaction to live Musik
  • Zero preparation, plug in and play
  • Perfect for concerts, parties, spontaneous events
  • Show generation for inspiration
  • Autonomous management during the event

Manual Programming

  • Full control over every keyframe
  • Millisecond-precise synchronization
  • Ideal for theatre and rehearsed shows
  • Specific effects not generable by AI
  • Exportable and reproducible shows
  • Combinable with Play Light mode

MyStrow offers both approaches in the same software. Start in AI mode, switch to Manual or Program for moments that require precise lighting. All 4 modes can be switched instantly during the show.

Use Cases

When to Use KI-Beleuchtung?

🎸

Rock & Electronic Concert

Powerful bass, high Tempo, variable energy: the AI generates flashes, strobes and Farbe changes in phase with the Musik. Zero intervention during the set.

🎷

Jazz & Acoustic Music

The AI adapts its algorithms: smooth transitions, warm coppery palettes, moderate intensities for an enveloping atmosphere.

🕺

DJ Night & Club

The AI automatically follows transitions, drops and builds, allowing the stage manager to focus on other aspects while maintaining a coherent light show.

📺

TV Sets & Streaming

The AI maintains dynamic lighting during Musikal sequences without mobilizing a dedicated operator. Its consistency guarantees a professional result every take.
