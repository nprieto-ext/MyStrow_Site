Show control software

All in one: sound, image & lighting

MyStrow brings together in a single interface what stage managers typically handle with multiple applications: music playback, video projection and lighting control.

Supported formats: MP3, WAV, FLAC, AAC, OGG for audio; MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV for video, projected on a second screen. Each playlist entry can be an audio file, video, manual pause or timed tempo — each with its own lighting mode and show on the timeline.

Reorder with drag-and-drop, navigate with keyboard shortcuts (PageUp/PageDown). Everything is stored in a single .tui file.

Features

A player built for live performance

🎵

High-fidelity audio playback

Built on Qt Multimedia, the audio engine delivers high-quality playback with minimal latency. Individual volume per track and independent master volume control.

📺

Video projection

Videos are projected full-screen on a second monitor, independently from the control interface. Perfect for shows combining music, video and lighting.

📋

Playlist management

Import and reorder by drag-and-drop, insert manual or automatic pauses. Audio, video, pause or timed tempo: your playlist is your full show rundown.

💡

Lighting mode per track

Each playlist entry has its own lighting mode: Manual, AI Lighting or Programme. MyStrow activates the right mode on every track change, automatically.

Sequencer

3 lighting modes for every moment of the show

1
Manual
Direct control from the AKAI APC mini mk2 or the UI. Full creative freedom in real time.
2
AI Lighting
The AI analyzes the audio and automatically generates colors, intensities and transitions.
3
Programme
Plays back pre-recorded keyframes for perfectly reproducible lighting at every performance.
Integration

Perfect light & sound synchronization

The player and the DMX lighting engine share the same time base: lighting changes arrive at exactly the right moment relative to the music.

In AI Lighting mode, audio feeds directly into spectral and rhythmic analysis, converted into DMX commands with no perceptible latency. In Programme mode, pre-recorded keyframes trigger in sync with the track.
