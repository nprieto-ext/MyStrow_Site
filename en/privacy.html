Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data collected

MyStrow collects only the data strictly necessary for licence activation: your email address and a machine identifier (machine ID) generated locally on your computer.

No browsing data, telemetry or show content is collected or transmitted.

2. Use of data

Collected data is used exclusively for licence verification. Verification is performed via Firebase Auth (Google) when the software starts. Your information is not used for advertising or commercial purposes.

3. Storage

Licence information is stored locally on your machine in an encrypted file (~/.maestro_account.dat), protected by Fernet encryption linked to your machine identifier. It is not accessible by third parties.

4. Your rights

You have the right to access, rectify and delete your data. To exercise these rights or request deletion of your account and associated data, contact us at the address below.

5. Contact

For any question regarding the protection of your personal data: nicolas@mystrow.fr
