The Software

Everything Inside MyStrow

⚡

Real-time DMX control

MyStrow sends your DMX commands 25 times per second via Art-Net. Every color or intensity change is applied immediately on stage.

🗂️

6 fixture groups

Organize your devices into 6 independent groups (A to F). Each group receives color, intensity and effects separately.

🌐

Up to 4 DMX universes

Control up to 2048 channels simultaneously across 4 independent universes. Ideal for large stages with many devices.

✨

AI Lighting

Enable AI and let it drive the whole stage. It analyzes sound in real time and automatically generates colors, intensities and transitions.

🎬

Timeline sequencer

Compose your shows in advance on a visual timeline. Color and intensity keyframes, drag-and-drop editable clips.

🎵

Audio & video player

Manage your playlist and projections from the same interface. Sound, image and light united in a single software.
