Show Editor

The MyStrow Timeline

The MyStrow timeline organizes your DMX sequences as colored clips on tracks each corresponding to a fixture group, like a video editor dedicated to lighting.

Each clip contains its own keyframes. A keyframe defines color (RGB/RGBW), intensity and interpolation curve at a given point in time. The millisecond-graduated time ruler enables precise synchronization with your music.

Drag-and-drop lets you move, resize and reorganize clips. Clips can be duplicated to reuse identical sequences at different moments in the show.

Features

Everything to Compose Professional Light Shows

📎

Editable Clips

Resize, move, duplicate your clips with drag-and-drop. Each clip contains as many keyframes as needed and chains with smooth transitions.

🔑

Precise Keyframes

Each keyframe captures RGB/RGBW color, intensity from 0 to 100% and interpolation type. Transitions are calculated and played in real time.

🎵

Music Synchronization

The light timeline is attached to each media file. When a track starts, MyStrow automatically loads the corresponding sequences. No offset — light follows music.

💾

Import & Export

Your shows are saved in .tui format (JSON), attached to the corresponding media. Share them with colleagues or reload them in one click on any MyStrow workstation.

Visual Feedback

Real-time Visualization

Every keyframe change is immediately reflected on the physical fixtures. No separate preview: Art-Net communication is always active, even in edit mode. MyStrow supports up to 4 Art-Net universes (2,048 channels).

The interactive stage plot (side panel or full screen) displays your fixtures in real time based on the DMX values sent, to check your sequences without being on stage.

Available programming modes

  • Manual mode: direct AKAI, StreamDeck or tablet control
  • AI Lighting mode: sound-reactive lighting
  • Program mode: pre-recorded keyframes
  • Play Light mode: synchronized timeline
  • Live recording of Manual actions
  • Combination of multiple modes per track
Workflow

From Rehearsal to Show

🎬

Live Recording

Enable recording and play in Manual mode with the AKAI, StreamDeck or a tablet. Every action is captured as keyframes on the timeline, giving a programming base from real artistic decisions.

✏️

Editing and Refinement

Fix timings, adjust colors, smooth transitions. Zoom to millisecond level to position keyframes precisely, then replay to validate.

📋

Playlist Organization

Organize media and shows in show order. Each entry has its own light mode and parameters. Lighting automatically adapts to each track change.

▶️

Show Playback

Launch the playlist in Play Light mode. MyStrow plays sequences synchronized with each track. You retain full control at any time via the AKAI, StreamDeck, a tablet or another mode.
